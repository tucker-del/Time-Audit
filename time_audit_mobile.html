HOW TO USE — under 5 min/day
Set your hours once. Tap any block throughout the day, pick a category, type 3 words, hit SAVE. Log in real-time or fill gaps at lunch and end of day.

CATEGORIES

CEO — Strategy, decisions, partnerships. Only things you can do.
SALES — FB Marketplace, GHL follow-up, outbound calls, closing.
OPS — Deliveries, yard work, vendor calls, logistics.
ADMIN — QBO, invoices, email, scheduling. VA territory.
PERSONAL — Meals, family, exercise. Non-negotiable.
DEAD — Scrolling, distracted, reactive. The number to kill.

HORMOZI'S 5 QUESTIONS — ASK FOR EVERY BLOCK

1.Can someone else do this? → Outsource it.
2.Does this directly make money? → If no, justify it or cut it.
3.Is this my highest-leverage use of time? → If no, stop.
4.Am I reacting or executing? → Reactive = DEAD time.
5.Would I pay $100/hr for this? → That's your CEO floor.

TARGETS

CEO≥ 30% of working hours
SALES≥ 25% of working hours
OPS≤ 20% (until you have a driver)
ADMIN≤ 10% — VA should own this
DEADGoal: zero
